Join us once more for a week of general relativity and gravitation studies! 

The III São Paulo School on Gravitational Physics is an initiative led by early-career researchers to explore and promote the various aspects of gravity. In 2026, we will adventure through topics ranging from celestial holography to the astrophysics of compact objects. We will also celebrate the fiftieth anniversay of the Unruh effect through a few special minicourses in preparation for the [Thermality in Quantum Field Theory in Curved Spacetimes](https://www.ictp-saifr.org/tqftcs2026/) conference, which will take place in São Paulo one week after the school.
